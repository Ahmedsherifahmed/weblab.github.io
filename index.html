Dr. Nur Safwati - Mechanical Robotics enthusiast

Nur Safwati Mohd Nor is currently a Senior at the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM. She pursued her doctoral studies at the Human-Robot Interaction Laboratory, Shibaura Institute of Technology, Tokyo, Japan, from 2011 to 2014. Her doctoral thesis, titled "Automatic Initialization System for Home-based Robotics Service Environment," reflects her profound dedication to unraveling complex problems situated at the convergence of mechanical, electrical, and software engineering, with particular interests in human-robot interaction, intelligent systems, and artificial intelligence. She can be contacted at nursafwati@utm.my
