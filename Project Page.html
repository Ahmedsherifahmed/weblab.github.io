INTELLIGENT MEDICAL DIAGNOSIS SYSTEM USING WOUND AND FACIAL ANALYSIS

Many patients nowadays struggle to obtain and understand basic information relating to their own health, due to the complexity of modern health care systems. Patient engagement encourages patients to improve their health outcomes by themselves and results in better patient care at lower costs. We propose an intelligent diagnosis system using deep learning techniques that can identify and classify user input in the form of images to encourage patient engagement.
